My Favorite Artist

I don't exactly have favorite artist, because I feel like I may change my mind any second. But thinking about
it, I catch myself on thought that I listen mostly to those guys, Twenty One Pilots, musicial duo I listen to mostly.
The group was formed in 2009 by lead vocalist Tyler Joseph along with Nick Thomas and Chris Salih, who both left in 2011.
Since their departure, the line-up has consisted of Tyler Joseph and drummer Josh Dun. Those guys created a story by writing
songs. You can find more about their lore online but in short The lore of Twenty One Pilots' songs centers on the fictional world
of Dema, a city ruled by nine Bishops who promote a religion of "Vialism." The story of Twenty One Pilots is often interpreted as
a metaphor for mental health struggles, particularly depression and anxiety.

Some their songs that I listen to often lately are:

  • Tear in My Heart
  • Drum Show
  • Downstairs
  • Days lie Dormant
  • Shy Away
